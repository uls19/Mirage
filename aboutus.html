Company Profile

Shandong Mirage Tyres Co., Ltd., is one of the famous tire manufacturers in China located in the tire capital town â€“ Guangrao which is in Dongying city of Shandong province. Shandong Mirage Tyres Co., Ltd. specialized in the production of full range of radial tires by international advanced technology. The factory has two plants with an annual production capacity of 2 million for TBR and 6 million for PCR products.

Mirage insists on technical innovation and uses the national technical center as the development platform. It has the independent R & D capabilities for all the relevant technologies of radial tires , Mirage manufactures radial tires widely used in passenger car, SUV, truck and bus etc., with excellent performance, which are well-reputed all over China and more than 80 countries overseas.

Mirage owns the advanced R & D, production and testing equipments, furthermore, the excellent management team and specialized technology troops , which assures the outstanding and stable quality of its products. Many of Mirage's manufacturing equipment are imported from Europe and Japan

Mirage has built a strong quality assurance system and its products are accredited with certificates of ISO9001, ISO14001, OHSAS18001.IATF16949, CCC, DOT, ECE, GCC, INMETRO, BIS, SNI, SONCAP, etc.
